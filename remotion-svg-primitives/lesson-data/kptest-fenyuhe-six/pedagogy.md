# pedagogy — kptest-fenyuhe-six

lesson kind: **math-acquisition** (part–whole bonds for 6; the three splits + their 合 are facts the child must produce on demand, not an insight the picture reveals)
builds on: kptest-fenyuhe-five (5的分与合) — child already knows the routine; this lesson extends the routine to a new whole (6)
stage ceiling: **concrete** for every split beat (operate on six dots); **represent** for the routine reprise and the recap (read the split set as a unit). **Never symbolize** — no digits, no equation form, no +/−/=. (Per the brief's out-of-scope list.)
continuity: reuse the registered dot primitive and the 分/合 motion vocabulary established in 5的分与合 so the child recognizes the routine. The six dots are identity-invariant across the splits.
the one beat (per brief): six dots split into two groups — 1和5, 2和4, 3和3 — and 合 back each time. The 3和3 equal split is the highlight.

## Reinforcement plan (state once, per §8)

- **Per-split acquisition.** Each of 1和5, 2和4, 3和3 is a fact the child must produce on demand. Each gets a model (voice names the bond) + a learner-response wait-time gap (held silence for the child to answer back).
- **Co-equal airtime.** All three splits earn the same shape (one model, one wait). The 3和3 highlight earns **extra dwell on its symmetric hold**, NOT extra cues — to preserve co-equal cue counts across the three routines.
- **Voice names the bond fully** (the §4 acquisition carve-out). The complete utterances "6可以分成1和5" / "1和5合成6" name the whole being decomposed — never a fragment like "一和五" spoken without its whole. The picture delivers the parts (the dots that move).
- **Wait-time floor.** ≥3–5s of held silence after every model, dedicated cue, for the child to answer before the next beat advances.
- **Spaced recall.** A recap beat at the end re-presents all three splits in order so the child retrieves (not just re-watches) the set.
- **Discovery-pacing note.** 9 discovery beats → fits the 60–90s band at early-childhood pace (~3–4s per acquisition cue + wait-times). Cue boundaries and IDs are owned by W1 (storyboard) — this list is the discovery spine, not the final cue count. W1 may bundle a split+合 model into one cue (picture does both motions, voice names the pair) or split them, as the choreography requires.

## Per-cue discovery list

### Cue 1 — Routine reprise (setup beat, §1 fourth shape)
discovery: 6 will be split the same way 5 was — the child recognizes the 分/合 routine from 5的分与合 transferring to a new whole.
stage: represent
focal: the six dots appear together as one whole (the row the child knows from the prior lesson, scaled to 6)
reinforcement: none (insight/setup; one pass is enough — the next 6 beats are the actual content)

### Cue 2 — Split 1和5 cycle (model)
discovery: 6 can be split into 1 and 5, and 1 and 5 combine back into 6 — a new bond the child can produce on demand.
stage: concrete
focal: one dot separates from the other five; the two groups then recombine into the six (split + 合 back as one continuous motion sequence)
reinforcement: model — voice says "6可以分成1和5；1和5合成6"; picture delivers the parts; the cue ends with a learner-response wait.

### Cue 3 — Wait-time after 1和5
discovery: the child retrieves the 1和5 bond by saying it back to themselves.
stage: concrete
focal: held silence; the 1-and-5 grouping remains on screen as the prompt
reinforcement: ≥3–5s learner-response gap; cue 2's model is the implicit prompt.

### Cue 4 — Split 2和4 cycle (model)
discovery: 6 can be split into 2 and 4, and 2 and 4 combine back into 6.
stage: concrete
focal: two dots separate from the other four; the groups recombine
reinforcement: model + wait (same shape as cue 2; co-equal — no compression).

### Cue 5 — Wait-time after 2和4
discovery: the child retrieves the 2和4 bond.
stage: concrete
focal: held silence; the 2-and-4 grouping remains on screen
reinforcement: ≥3–5s learner-response gap.

### Cue 6 — Split 3和3 cycle (model) — HIGHLIGHTED equal split
discovery: 6 can be split exactly in half — 3 and 3 — and 3 and 3 combine back into 6; the equal split is a new property of 6 felt as symmetry.
stage: concrete
focal: the six dots break exactly down the middle; the two groups are visually mirror-symmetric
reinforcement: model + wait (same cue count as 1和5 and 2和4 — co-equal); **extra dwell** on the symmetric 3-3 hold so the equality registers (extra seconds, not extra cues — preserves co-equal airtime per the §8 "breadth before depth" rule).

### Cue 7 — Wait-time after 3和3
discovery: the child retrieves the 3和3 equal-split bond.
stage: concrete
focal: held silence; the symmetric 3-3 grouping remains on screen longer than the prior two wait-time holds
reinforcement: ≥3–5s learner-response gap; the symmetric pairing's extra dwell carries into this hold.

### Cue 8 — Aggregator prompt (spaced retrieval of the set)
discovery: the child retrieves the **set** of all three splits of 6 — 1和5, 2和4, 3和3 — in response to one prompt, demonstrating the part–whole structure as a whole.
stage: represent
focal: the six dots reassembled as a single whole; the child is invited to name the splits aloud
reinforcement: ≥3–5s wait on the prompt "6可以分成几和几?"; the child supplies the set; co-equal retrieval across all three splits (none starved — a 1和5-only or 3和3-only answer is partial).

### Cue 9 — Recap / spaced recall
discovery: the child confirms the part–whole structure of 6 by seeing (or naming) all three splits in order, completing the routine.
stage: represent
focal: the three splits replayed in canonical order — 1和5, then 2和4, then 3和3 — closing the loop to the routine reprise in cue 1
reinforcement: spaced recall (cues 2 / 4 / 6 retrieved here, ~30–60s after their model); no new content; the recap is the lesson's closing integration, not a new delivery.

## Notes for downstream waves

- **Stage audit (§3).** No cue reaches symbolize. No digits (6, 1, 5, 2, 4, 3) appear as numerals on screen; counts are in the dot arrays and in the spoken Chinese. (The brief's out-of-scope line — "No +/−/= symbols" — is honored at the stage level, not just by element absence.)
- **No leakage (§4 carve-out).** For these acquisition targets, the voice **names the fact** ("6分成1和5") and the picture **delivers the concrete instance** (the dots separating). The §4 rule against the narrator pre-announcing a count the child is meant to perform does not bite here — the child is not *counting*; the child is *retrieving a memorized bond*.
- **Co-equal audit (§8).** Cues 2, 4, 6 are the three split-model beats; they have identical structure. The 3和3 highlight is expressed as longer dwell on its hold (cue 7), not as a fourth model cue. Cue 8's aggregator prompt explicitly accepts the full set, so a child who can only name 1和5 is visibly short of the goal — the design itself enforces co-equal retrieval.
- **Conservation is shown, not stated.** The 合 motion in cues 2, 4, 6 is the picture's job (the groups recombine); the voice names the symmetric bond "X和Y合成6" alongside the model. Conservation of the whole is *seen*, not lectured.
