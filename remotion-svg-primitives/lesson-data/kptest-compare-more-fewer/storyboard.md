# storyboard — kptest-compare-more-fewer

lesson kind: math-insight (paired picture reveals MORE/FEWER) WITH an L2-style acquisition layer — 五比三多 / 三比五少 are voiced acquisition targets the child hears, copies, and retrieves; the matching insight is picture-delivered (voice never pre-names which group has more).

Lesson shape: one intro card → a single SET-UP picture (5 red over 3 blue) that is NEVER re-laid-out, so the whole spine is read off ONE invariant pairing. The one-to-one match reveals the surplus before any word for it (`reveal`). Each direction then gets a clean acquisition triad — model the full 比-utterance on the surplus, invite the echo with its own wait-time cue, replay once — keeping 多 and 少 co-equal (§8 breadth). The keystone `same-picture-two-readings` is honored by NEVER changing the dots between `more-direction*` and `fewer-direction*` (only the reading focus slides). One insight-guard cue proves spreading the dots wide does not change who has more. One closing retrieval recap walks both targets on the original picture. The picture carries the math; the voice carries the two phrases.

Beat-ordering note for composer (binding, from `announce-topic.requires`): in `intro` the title/teaser reads ALONE first; the dot cast enters only in `two-groups`, never overlaid on the title.

Invariant-picture note for composer (from pedagogy keystone): the SAME dot layout + the SAME pair lines established in `match` are HELD unchanged through `more-direction`, `echo-more`, `fewer-direction`, `echo-fewer`. No re-layout, no redraw of the pairing between those cues — only the reading-focus / surplus pulse / comparison-symbol differs. `not-by-size` is the ONLY cue that moves dots (spread), and it re-pairs to the same surplus.

### intro
- **discovery ref:** none yet — announces the topic (谁多谁少 / comparing two groups) so the child knows what this video is for.
- **stage:** represent
- **teaching action(s):** `announce-topic`
- **narration beat (intent):** name the lesson — comparing two groups to find which has more and which has fewer; teaser only, no target phrase yet.
- **required visual:** `lesson-intro-card` (title + section eyebrow + one-line KP teaser), reading alone; no dots present yet.

### two-groups
- **discovery ref:** here are two groups of dots — five red on top, three blue below — laid in two aligned rows; nothing is compared yet, the child just takes in "two groups, different amounts."
- **stage:** concrete
- **teaching action(s):** `stage-the-moment`
- **narration beat (intent):** present two groups and name each amount (five up here, three down here); withhold the comparison entirely.
- **required visual:** two aligned rows of `countable-object` dots (5 in group-A color top, 3 in group-B color below) — identical dots so only COUNT differs (color = group ID, not size); rows top-aligned by column so a future one-to-one drop reads straight down.

### match
- **discovery ref:** when each top dot drops a line straight down to one bottom dot, two top dots are left with NO partner — the child SEES the surplus; the leftover pair is the proof, before any word for it.
- **stage:** represent
- **teaching action(s):** `reveal`
- **narration beat (intent):** narrate the ACTION only ("each top dot looks for a partner below"); let the picture deliver the two left over — do NOT say "five is more."
- **required visual:** `pair-connector` lines growing top→bottom one-to-one (three pairs complete), then the two unpaired top dots marked with `unmatched-slot` (state ghost) — a dashed ghost partner under each surplus dot so "more" reads as "these two have nobody."

### more-direction
- **discovery ref:** the two leftover dots have a name: 五比三多 — five is more than three. The acquisition target is the WHOLE utterance (names both groups + direction), bound to the surplus the child just saw.
- **stage:** symbolize
- **teaching action(s):** `model-target-slow → track-read-along`
- **narration beat (intent):** model the COMPLETE comparison utterance — the five-group, the three-group, and the 多 direction in one breath (the surplus-pair the picture is pulsing IS the referent); slow, isolated, said twice.
- **required visual:** the held pairing with the two surplus dots pulsing (`pulse-circle` on the unmatched pair); a `comparison-symbol` (symbol `>`, style formal) attaching the top group over the bottom group; `read-along-highlight` sweeping the phrase row token-by-token on the currently-spoken token.

### echo-more
- **discovery ref:** the two leftover dots have a name: 五比三多 — five is more than three. The acquisition target is the WHOLE utterance (names both groups + direction), bound to the surplus the child just saw.
- **stage:** symbolize
- **teaching action(s):** `invite-echo → learner-response-gap`
- **narration beat (intent):** invite the child to say the WHOLE utterance back ("跟我说：[the five-more-than-three comparison]"), then a held silent your-turn beat before anything advances.
- **required visual:** a clear "your turn" affordance held through the silence (`pointer-hand-arrow` variant hand at the phrase / a turn-cue), the same surplus picture unchanged; the phrase row stays legible through the gap.

### more-replay
- **discovery ref:** the two leftover dots have a name: 五比三多 — five is more than three. The acquisition target is the WHOLE utterance (names both groups + direction), bound to the surplus the child just saw.
- **stage:** symbolize
- **teaching action(s):** `replay` — `replay of more-direction`
- **narration beat (intent):** meet the full 五比三多 utterance once more, identically, on the same surplus picture (cheap consistent reinforcement; reuse the `more-direction` clip + visual).
- **required visual:** identical to `more-direction` (same pulse on the surplus pair, same `comparison-symbol` >, same `read-along-highlight`).

### fewer-direction
- **discovery ref:** THE SAME paired picture, read the other way, is 三比五少 — three is fewer than five. Nothing in the picture changes; only the direction of the reading flips. This is the keystone: 多 and 少 are two names for ONE comparison.
- **stage:** symbolize
- **teaching action(s):** `reveal → model-target-slow → track-read-along`
- **narration beat (intent):** keystone — voice that the SAME picture, read the other way, is the COMPLETE three-fewer-than-five comparison (names both groups + the 少 direction); say it slowly, twice; the reveal is that no dot moved, only the reading flipped.
- **required visual:** the IDENTICAL pairing held (no re-layout — same dots, same `pair-connector` lines), the reading-focus sliding from the surplus (多) onto the short bottom row (少); `comparison-symbol` re-reads bottom-vs-top as the 少 direction; `read-along-highlight` on the new phrase row. Longest dwell of the lesson.

### echo-fewer
- **discovery ref:** THE SAME paired picture, read the other way, is 三比五少 — three is fewer than five. Nothing in the picture changes; only the direction of the reading flips. This is the keystone: 多 and 少 are two names for ONE comparison.
- **stage:** symbolize
- **teaching action(s):** `invite-echo → learner-response-gap`
- **narration beat (intent):** invite the child to say the WHOLE three-fewer-than-five utterance back, then a held silent your-turn beat before advancing.
- **required visual:** "your turn" affordance (`pointer-hand-arrow` hand at the short-row phrase) held through the silence; the same unchanged picture; phrase row legible through the gap.

### fewer-replay
- **discovery ref:** THE SAME paired picture, read the other way, is 三比五少 — three is fewer than five. Nothing in the picture changes; only the direction of the reading flips. This is the keystone: 多 and 少 are two names for ONE comparison.
- **stage:** symbolize
- **teaching action(s):** `replay` — `replay of fewer-direction`
- **narration beat (intent):** meet the full 三比五少 utterance once more, identically, on the same unchanged picture (reuse the `fewer-direction` clip + visual).
- **required visual:** identical to `fewer-direction` (same held pairing, same short-row focus, same `comparison-symbol`, same `read-along-highlight`).

### not-by-size
- **discovery ref:** which group has MORE is decided by matching, NOT by how spread out the dots are — spreading the three blue dots wide so the row looks longer does not make them more; re-matching still leaves the five with two over.
- **stage:** represent
- **teaching action(s):** `reveal`
- **narration beat (intent):** ask the question ("看起来变多了吗 / does the longer-looking row have more now?"); let the picture DELIVER "no — re-match and the five still has two over." Do not pre-answer.
- **required visual:** the three bottom dots spreading WIDER (row visually longer), then `pair-connector` re-pairing one-to-one — still two top dots with `unmatched-slot` ghosts. The surplus is invariant under spreading.

### recap
- **discovery ref:** retrieval — both utterances come back on the original picture: 五比三多 AND 三比五少 name the same paired groups; the child re-produces both, closing the loop.
- **stage:** symbolize
- **teaching action(s):** `spaced-recall`
- **narration beat (intent):** retrieve BOTH co-equal targets as complete utterances on the original (un-spread) picture — prompt → brief say-it gap → the surplus lights and the five-more-than-three comparison comes back; then the short row lights and the three-fewer-than-five comparison comes back. Equal airtime; neither merely name-checked.
- **required visual:** `recap-spotlight` (recap stack, ONE live moving highlight): walk the surplus pair → the 五比三多 phrase row, then the short bottom row → the 三比五少 phrase row, the live highlight always on the currently-spoken target; original picture restored (dots back to the matched layout, not spread).
