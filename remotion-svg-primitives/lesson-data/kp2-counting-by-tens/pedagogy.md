lesson kind: math-insight

## bundle-recall
discovery:     The single bundle of sticks we learned about represents a single unified group called "一个十" (one ten).
stage:         represent
focal:         A single, pre-existing bundle of sticks highlighted in the center.
reinforcement: none (recall of the unit from the previous lesson to anchor today's concept)

## untie-reveal
discovery:     This unified group called "一个十" (one ten) is composed of exactly ten individual ones.
stage:         concrete
focal:         The rope around the bundle untying to reveal ten individual loose sticks.
reinforcement: none (insight, one pass; the physical transformation is the proof)

## slow-count-ones
discovery:     Counting loose objects one by one is slow and repetitive because it requires ten separate counts.
stage:         concrete
focal:         The sequence of highlights and small ordinal markers incrementing slowly from 1 to 10 across the loose sticks.
reinforcement: none (the slow pacing itself builds the felt need for a faster unit)

## fast-vs-slow
discovery:     Bundling ten loose ones together lets us count them as a single group in only one count instead of ten.
stage:         represent
focal:         The ten individual sticks grouping and being tied back into a bundle as a "1" badge appears next to it.
reinforcement: none (insight, one pass; direct contrast with the slow-count cue delivers the discovery)

## two-tens
discovery:     We can count bundles of tens using the same counting sequence as ones: adding one more bundle gives us "两个十" (two tens).
stage:         represent
focal:         A second identical bundle sliding in atomically next to the first bundle, with the count badge advancing to "2" (两个十).
reinforcement: first step of establishing the count-by-tens sequence

## three-tens
discovery:     The same counting pattern continues: adding another bundle increments the count to "三个十" (three tens) while each bundle still holds ten ones inside.
stage:         represent
focal:         A third identical bundle sliding in atomically next to the others, with the count badge advancing to "3" (三个十).
reinforcement: second step of establishing the pattern, reinforcing the count-by-tens sequence through replication

## recap
discovery:     Counting by tens is much faster and more convenient than counting by ones when we have many objects.
stage:         represent
focal:         The final layout showing three bundles together with their count indicators "1", "2", "3" (三个十) clearly visualised.
reinforcement: spaced recall of the comparison to summarize and solidify the core insight
