lesson kind: math-insight

discovery:     The child discovers that five can be split into one and four.
stage:         concrete
focal:         The five dots separating into two groups (one dot and four dots).
reinforcement: one pass (modeled once, will be recalled in spaced-recap)

discovery:     The child discovers that when one and four are combined, the total is still five (conservation).
stage:         concrete
focal:         The two groups (one dot and four dots) moving together to form one group of five.
reinforcement: one pass (modeled once, will be recalled in spaced-recap)

discovery:     The child discovers that five can also be split into two and three.
stage:         concrete
focal:         The five dots separating into two groups (two dots and three dots).
reinforcement: one pass (modeled once, will be recalled in spaced-recap)

discovery:     The child discovers that when two and three are combined, the total is still five (conservation).
stage:         concrete
focal:         The two groups (two dots and three dots) moving together to form one group of five.
reinforcement: one pass (modeled once, will be recalled in spaced-recap)

discovery:     The child discovers that they can recall how to split five into two parts (they actively retrieve the splits from memory).
stage:         concrete
focal:         The wait-time silence after the question, during which the child thinks.
reinforcement: learner-response gap with wait-time ≥3s, then reveal

discovery:     The child discovers that one correct split is one and four (or two and three) — the answer to the question.
stage:         concrete
focal:         The appearance of the one-and-four split (or the highlighting of that split) as the revealed answer.
reinforcement: none (insight, one pass)

discovery:     The child discovers that both splits (1和4 and 2和3) are valid ways to split five, reinforcing the relations.
stage:         concrete
focal:         The rapid display of both splits in sequence (or side-by-side).
reinforcement: spaced recall (two exposures)
