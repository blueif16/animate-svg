## KEY DECISIONS
- Chose to structure the lesson with an announce-topic cue, five bond cues (one for each number bond: 9+1, 8+2, 7+3, 6+4, 5+5), and a recap cue.
- Each bond cue chains three teaching actions: invite-echo (to pose the question), learner-response-gap (to wait for child's response), and reveal (to show the missing dots sliding in and deliver the insight).
- The recap cue uses the spaced-recall teaching action to review the bonds without a response gap.
- Defined acquisition targets as the initial number of dots shown in the ten-frame (9, 8, 7, 6, 5), each encountered twice (once in the bond cue, once in the recap) for the exposures ledger.
