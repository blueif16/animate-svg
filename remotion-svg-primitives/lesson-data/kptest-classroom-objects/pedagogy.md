lesson kind: language/L2

discovery:     The child discovers that the English word for the pencil object is "pencil".
stage:         represent
focal:         The pencil icon lifting up from the pencil-case.
reinforcement: The child hears the word as the object lifts (model), then a choral invitation "跟我说：pencil", followed by a 3-second wait-time gap for the child to attempt the word, then the teacher models again. This cued exposure is balanced with pen and ruler.

focal:         The pen icon lifting up from the pencil-case.
reinforcement: The child hears the word as the object lifts (model), then a choral invitation "跟我说：pen", followed by a 3-second wait-time gap for the child to attempt the word, then the teacher models again. This cued exposure is balanced with pen and ruler.

discovery:     The child discovers that the English word for the ruler object is "ruler".
stage:         represent
focal:         The ruler icon lifting up from the pencil-case.
reinforcement: The child hears the word as the object lifts (model), then a choral invitation "跟我说：ruler", followed by a 3-second wait-time gap for the child to attempt the word, then the teacher models again. Due to the /r/ and final schwa being harder, an additional echo choral cue is added after the wait-time gap.

discovery:     The child discovers that they can recall and say the English names for all three objects when shown together.
stage:         represent
focal:         The three icons appearing together in a row.
reinforcement: A spaced-recall beat: the teacher points to each icon in turn and invites the child to say the word, with a brief wait-time after each point. This serves as retrieval practice for all three words equally.
