# kptest-first-second-third

**Audience.** 4–6岁, Mandarin Chinese (zh)
**Length.** 50–70 seconds band — HINT for scope, not a target
**Builds on.** none
**Style.** default

## Knowledge point
The child leaves knowing the first three ordinal words — 第一、第二、第三 — and can answer "谁排第几?" about a short queue: position in a line maps to its ordinal word, counted from the front.

## The one beat
Three animals line up one at a time. As each one settles into the line, the queue is counted FROM THE FRONT and the just-settled animal receives its ordinal label — 第一, then 第二, then 第三. Then the line is asked one retrieval question (e.g. 谁排第二?) and the matching animal steps forward.

## Out of scope
Cardinal counting beyond three (no 四 or higher); no more/fewer comparison; no written digits as teaching objects; counting from the back / direction edge cases.

## Continuity
none (fresh test lesson)

## Narration notes
Ordinal targets are short Mandarin tokens — known Aoede rate ~0.30s/Chinese-char applies; no other known TTS risks.
