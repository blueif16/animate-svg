# kp2-counting-by-tens — storyboard

Five cues, 1:1 with `pedagogy.md` Beats 1–5. Mandarin narration. Target total 35–55s.

Narration is written as intent in Mandarin (target language per brief). It prepares or names; the picture delivers the discovery (pedagogy §4). Visual-change descriptions are pedagogical — what visibly changes between cue start and cue end — not primitive picks, colors, durations, or frame counts. Those belong to Wave 2.

---

## cue: loose-count-felt

- **serves beat:** Beat 1 — Loose count, felt
- **narration intent (zh):** "我们一根一根地数。" (Optionally a soft trailing "…慢慢数" if pacing needs it.)
- **visual change:** Ten loose sticks are already on stage; a running count advances one tick at a time across them, reaching ten. The sticks themselves do not move — the *count* is what walks, and the child feels how many steps it took.
- **target duration band:** long — the labor has to be felt, not merely acknowledged. The cost of ten separate ticks is the discovery.

## cue: bundle-is-one-count

- **serves beat:** Beat 2 — The bundle is one count
- **narration intent (zh):** "这十根，捆成一捆 — 一个十。" (Narrator names "一个十" *after* the rope is visibly tied, never before.)
- **visual change:** The same ten loose sticks collapse into a single bundle, and the running count display flips from ten ticks down to one count of a new unit ("一个十"). The same ten things, recounted as one.
- **target duration band:** medium — the collapse from ten-count to one-count is the punchline of the first half; it needs room to land but not to linger.

## cue: tens-count-like-ones

- **serves beat:** Beat 3 — Tens count like ones
- **narration intent (zh):** "再来一捆 — 两个十。"
- **visual change:** A second bundle enters the scene (atomically, already tied — never re-animate the bundling). The tens-count advances from 1 → 2. "一个十, 两个十" is now spoken-and-shown.
- **target duration band:** medium — single bundle entry plus the tens-count tick; the child must have time to see the count advance before the next bundle.

## cue: pattern-holds

- **serves beat:** Beat 4 — The pattern holds
- **narration intent (zh):** "再加一捆 — 三个十。"
- **visual change:** A third bundle enters; the tens-count advances 2 → 3. The third instance is what confirms the rule generalizes — three bundles now sit in a short row, each carrying its own ordinal count of the new unit.
- **target duration band:** medium — same shape as the previous cue. The child should feel the *sameness* of the operation, not be hurried through it.

## cue: tens-are-the-faster-way

- **serves beat:** Beat 5 — Why this is the faster way
- **narration intent (zh):** "数 '一' 要走十步，数 '十' 只走三步。" (Narrator names the count-lengths only — does not say "twenty," "thirty," or any digit-symbol.)
- **visual change:** The long row of loose ones and the short row of three bundles come into view side by side, each carrying its own count-length. The difference in row-length is the visible argument: same total, far fewer counts on the bundle side.
- **target duration band:** long — the comparison is the closing image; the child needs a hold to see both rows at once and absorb the speed difference.

---

## Discipline notes (for downstream waves)

- Narration is intent only; Wave 2b owns final phrasing and Wave 3 owns timing.
- Brief out-of-scope is honored: no digit "10/20/30" anywhere on screen or in narration, no 十位/个位, no 计数单位 said aloud, no re-teaching the bundling action (bundles 2 and 3 arrive already tied).
- The spoken count and the visible count match at every cue (pedagogy §7): cue 1 shows ten ticks while ten counts are spoken; cue 2 shows "一个十" only after the rope ties; cues 3–4 show the tens-count advancing as the narrator names the new ordinal; cue 5 names "十步" / "三步" only when both rows are visible.
- Five cues, not eight. The brief's "~8 cues at slow pace" estimate is a length-budget guideline, not a target — adding cues that don't deliver a new discovery would violate pedagogy §1. If 35s lands short of the band, Wave 2b can extend per-cue holds rather than invent extra cues.
