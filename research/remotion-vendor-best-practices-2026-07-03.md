# Remotion vendor best-practices study (2026-07-03)

Synthesis of 13 external vendor scans + our own self-scan (`research/_vendor-scan/*.md`, one file per repo, all citations below lifted verbatim from those notes). Scope: two questions ŌĆö how the field prevents layout/overlay defects, and how it keeps voice and visuals in sync ŌĆö and what our lesson pipeline should absorb.

## 1. Executive summary

**Layout ŌĆö three cross-repo conclusions.**
1. **Nobody in the field ships collision detection; everybody prevents by construction.** Grep-proven zero bbox/overlap checking in 12 of 13 vendors ŌĆö safety comes from flow slots + reserved zones + residual arithmetic (`vendor/skills/skills/remotion/rules/video-layout.md:30-38`, `vendor/recorder/remotion/layout/get-webcam-size.ts:7-41`). Our measured `getBBox` gate is unique in the field; the field's construction-first doctrine is its missing complement, not its rival.
2. **Text is the field's solved problem and our largest gap.** Every first-party Remotion codebase measures text with real font metrics before rendering (`vendor/remotion/packages/layout-utils/src/layouts/fit-text-on-n-lines.ts:20-101`, `vendor/recorder/remotion/captions/processing/layout-captions.ts:71-158`); we have grep-proven zero `fitText`/`measureText` anywhere.
3. **The numbers exist and are encodable.** 80px/100px safe areas and 84/44/32px type floors at 1080w, width-scaled (`vendor/skills/skills/remotion/rules/video-layout.md:15`, confirmed by RemotionUI [web]) ŌĆö advisory prose everywhere in the field; only our gate infrastructure can make them machine checks.

**Audio sync ŌĆö three cross-repo conclusions.**
1. **Measured audio as the master clock is universal** ŌĆö 8+ repos derive composition length from probed audio in `calculateMetadata` (`vendor/template-audiogram/src/Root.tsx:42-62`); our Wave 3.5 `max(narration+gap, motion)+tail` is the strongest variant found anywhere (no vendor has a motion budget).
2. **Unit-local audio clips in their own `<Sequence>` is convergent evolution** ŌĆö 4 repos independently arrive at our v4 cue-anchored architecture (`vendor/remotion-superpowers/skills/remotion-production/rules/elevenlabs-advanced.md:95-120`, `vendor/claude-video-kit/remotion/src/Root.tsx:163-179`).
3. **Token-onset binding is the published consensus for any spoken-synced visual** (`vendor/template-tiktok/src/CaptionedVideo/Page.tsx:32,67-87`); where repos disagree ŌĆö WPM estimation vs ASR measurement (`vendor/remotion-superpowers/skills/remotion-production/rules/voiceover-sync.md:32-48`) ŌĆö measurement wins, full stop. The field ships onset binding live; we built the better API (`cueHelpers.ts`) and then didn't call it.

## 2. Layout & overlay playbook

Patterns ranked by prevalence ├Ś strength.

### L1. Measure-then-fit text sizing (fitText / fillTextBox / fitTextOnNLines, always with a cap)
- **Repos:** remotion (`vendor/remotion/packages/layout-utils/src/layouts/fit-text-on-n-lines.ts:20-101`, `vendor/remotion/packages/layout-utils/src/layouts/fill-text-box.ts:3-98`), template-tiktok (`vendor/template-tiktok/src/CaptionedVideo/Page.tsx:34-41`), template-audiogram (`vendor/template-audiogram/src/Audiogram/get-number-of-lines-for-text.ts:17-47`), recorder (`vendor/recorder/remotion/captions/processing/layout-captions.ts:71-158`), skills (`vendor/skills/skills/remotion/rules/measuring-text.md:20-83`).
- **Recipe:** For any data-driven string (title, caption, label): measure with the REAL font via `@remotion/layout-utils`. Single-line ŌåÆ `fitText({fontFamily, text, withinWidth})`, then `Math.min(cap, fitted)` ŌĆö the constant is only the ceiling. Multi-line ŌåÆ `fillTextBox({maxBoxWidth, maxLines})` fed word-by-word, break/paginate on `exceedsBox`; or `fitTextOnNLines` (binary search over fontSize against a full wrap simulation, returns both size AND the exact measured lines to render). Derive `maxLines` from the zone rect: `floor((boxHeight - pad) / (fontSize * LINE_HEIGHT))` with the SAME `LINE_HEIGHT` constant feeding measurement and CSS (`vendor/recorder/remotion/captions/boxed/components/CaptionSentence.tsx:33-46`). Post-process the cut for orphans/punctuation (`vendor/remotion/packages/captions/src/ensure-max-characters-per-line.ts:32-66`).
- **Fails when:** text is CJK ŌĆö `text.split(' ')` treats a Chinese sentence as one unbreakable word (remotion scan C3); `fitText`'s linear solve overshoots with fixed-px `letterSpacing`; uncapped fitText makes short strings comically large; and no repo feeds "fitted size below legibility floor" back into paging.

### L2. Font-loaded-before-measure gate (+ fallback-font assertion)
- **Repos:** recorder (`vendor/recorder/remotion/helpers/WaitForFonts.tsx:5-43`), template-audiogram (`vendor/template-audiogram/src/Audiogram/WaitForFonts.tsx:9-39`), template-tiktok (`vendor/template-tiktok/src/CaptionedVideo/index.tsx:69-79`), remotion (`vendor/remotion/packages/layout-utils/src/layouts/measure-text.ts:146-180`), skills (`vendor/skills/skills/remotion/rules/measuring-text.md:87-141`).
- **Recipe:** Block with `delayRender()` and mount nothing that measures text until `loadFont(...).waitUntilDone()` resolves ("Missing fonts can influence the layout calculation"). Pass `validateFontIsLoaded: true` ŌĆö remotion's 15-line differential assertion measures a second time with the fallback font and THROWS if the boxes are pixel-identical. Share one style object between the measure call and the rendered element; `outline` not `border`; `white-space: pre` in markup to match the measurer (`vendor/remotion/packages/docs/docs/layout-utils/best-practices.mdx:212-250`).
- **Fails when:** skipped ŌĆö measurements silently disagree between studio and render; the false-positive guard needs >4 unique chars.

### L3. Overlap prevention by construction: flow slots own space, absolute positioning = decoration only
- **Repos:** skills (`vendor/skills/skills/remotion/rules/video-layout.md:30-38`; `vendor/skills/skills/remotion/rules/sequencing.md:49-56`), remotion (`vendor/remotion/packages/skills/skills/remotion/rules/video-layout.md:30-38`), open-design (`vendor/open-design/plugins/_official/examples/hyperframes/SKILL.md:178-246`), remotion-superpowers (`vendor/remotion-superpowers/skills/remotion-production/rules/data-visualization.md:31-47,152-166`).
- **Recipe:** Every readable element gets a reserved slot in normal flex/grid flow with `gap`; absolute positioning is demoted to backgrounds/decoration that layer BEHIND content. Entrances animate FROM the reserved slot via opacity/transform/scale ŌĆö never into occupied space; offscreen entries still land in a slot. Unknown-length text gets wrap room, never a neighbor immediately below. Open-design's "Layout Before Animation": build the static hero-frame (max-simultaneous-elements) CSS FIRST, then add tweens as deltas from that known-good layout. In Remotion specifically: `<Sequence layout="none">` inside slotted containers ŌĆö the default AbsoluteFill wrapper silently converts every sequenced element into a full-frame overlay, the overlap-by-default failure mode. When a frame is crowded, fix temporally: reveal one after another, cut, or defer to another scene ŌĆö never shrink everything (`vendor/skills/skills/remotion/rules/video-layout.md:53-68`).
- **Fails when:** it stays prose ŌĆö nothing in any vendor enforces it (skills scan C: `fillTextBox` exists but no rule connects it back to the safe-area rules); remotion-cinematic built a full avoid-zone engine and then no production scene used it (`vendor/remotion-cinematic/src/engine/layout/zones.ts:38-140` ŌĆö built, tested, unused).

### L4. Numeric safe areas + per-role type floors, width-relative
- **Repos:** skills (`vendor/skills/skills/remotion/rules/video-layout.md:15` and `:44-48`), remotion-superpowers (`vendor/remotion-superpowers/skills/remotion-production/rules/captions-workflow.md:154-167`, `vendor/remotion-superpowers/commands/create-short.md:124-127`), short-video-maker (`vendor/short-video-maker/src/components/videos/PortraitVideo.tsx:38-48`), template-tiktok (`vendor/template-tiktok/src/CaptionedVideo/Page.tsx:15-21`); RemotionUI ships `getSafeAreaPadding()`/`scaleFont()` implementing the same numbers [web].
- **Recipe:** At 1080px width: key text Ōēź80px from sides, Ōēź100px from top/bottom; font minimums 84px headline / 44px supporting / 32px labels; captions in platform bands (bottom 15-25% TikTok-style, all text in center 60% for 9:16); Ōēź48px floor for mobile. Scale everything with composition width (`80/1080 * width`), never store as absolute px. Express placement as a small enum of pre-validated slots, not free coordinates.
- **Fails when:** hardcoded to one canvas (template-tiktok's `bottom: 350` band breaks at any other height ŌĆö its scan C2) or left as prose an agent may ignore (the skills repo's own caption demo violates its own 80px rule).

### L5. Residual-space zone arithmetic: one safe-space unit, budget the primary, siblings get the remainder
- **Repos:** recorder (`vendor/recorder/config/layout.ts:22-25`, `vendor/recorder/remotion/layout/get-webcam-size.ts:7-41`, `vendor/recorder/remotion/layout/get-captions-layout.ts:12-57`), github-unwrapped (`vendor/github-unwrapped/remotion/Issues/constants.ts:4-9`), claude-video-kit (`vendor/claude-video-kit/remotion/src/compositions/BrandedSlideLayout.tsx:60-141`).
- **Recipe:** One margin constant (`getSafeSpace() = 30`); every zone edge is `safeSpace ├Ś gapCount` (gaps counted, not eyeballed). Give the primary zone an explicit budget fraction (display Ōēż 3/5 height, Ōēż 0.77 width); size each sibling from the RESIDUAL: `canvas ŌłÆ primary ŌłÆ 3├ŚsafeSpace`. Captions get the band the webcam doesn't use ŌĆö the zone repositions itself when the layout flips. Fix chrome (brand line, counter, watermark) absolutely OUTSIDE the content flex tree in one shared wrapper. Make fit helpers throw loudly with the numbers when an element exceeds its container (`vendor/recorder/remotion/layout/fit-element.ts:6-36`).
- **Fails when:** residuals go negative unguarded (recorder's own gap: a tall display yields a negative-height caption box that renders garbage silently) ŌĆö pair with a measured gate.

### L6. Geometry as pure data, computed once before render
- **Repos:** recorder (`vendor/recorder/remotion/layout/get-layout.ts:278-316`, `vendor/recorder/remotion/animations/interpolate-layout.ts:4-62`), remotion-cinematic (`vendor/remotion-cinematic/src/engine/choreography/resolveWindowPose.ts:40-94`).
- **Recipe:** Every visual box is a plain `{left, top, width, height, borderRadius, opacity}` record computed in ONE pure module (inside `calculateMetadata` or a resolver) from declarative inputs; scene components only READ rects, never invent geometry. Transitions tween rectŌåÆrect between two already-valid layouts, so intermediate frames stay sane and identity-preserving movement falls out for free. Pose resolvers are pure `(def, frame) ŌåÆ pose` functions ŌĆö unit-testable with zero Remotion coupling.
- **Fails when:** the engine exists but scenes bypass it with hand-placed coordinates (remotion-cinematic's documented fate); rects must feed a verification pass or hand-tuning creeps back.

### L7. Composition-relative sizing tokens (one scalar, threaded everywhere)
- **Repos:** template-audiogram (`vendor/template-audiogram/src/Audiogram/constants.ts:1-5`, `vendor/template-audiogram/src/Audiogram/Main.tsx:29,40`), claude-video-kit (`vendor/claude-video-kit/remotion/src/presets.ts:31-84`), template-tiktok (`vendor/template-tiktok/src/CaptionedVideo/Page.tsx:31,37`), editor-pro-max (`vendor/editor-pro-max/src/components/backgrounds/GridPattern.tsx:15-107`), open-design (`vendor/open-design/apps/daemon/src/brands/engine/artifacts/deck.ts:58-159` ŌĆö cqi container-query units).
- **Recipe:** One `BASE_SIZE` (or preset `fontScale`) from which every font/line/gap derives as a multiple; safe widths as fractions of `useVideoConfig().width` (`width * 0.9`), and the IDENTICAL expression feeds both measurement and CSS so they cannot disagree. Decorative backgrounds derive extent from `useVideoConfig()`, never a hardcoded 1920├Ś1080.
- **Fails when:** partially applied ŌĆö vanta imports `useVideoConfig` then hardcodes `1920├Ś1080` in child SVGs anyway (its scan C); residual px literals undercut the token system (audiogram's own `48px` string duplicating BASE_SIZE).

### L8. Count-adaptive layout for repeated units
- **Repos:** github-unwrapped (`vendor/github-unwrapped/remotion/Issues/make-ufo-positions.tsx:35-49, 119-163`; `vendor/github-unwrapped/remotion/Productivity/Productivity.tsx:66-100`), remotion-superpowers (`vendor/remotion-superpowers/skills/remotion-production/rules/data-visualization.md:31-47,152-166`).
- **Recipe:** Column count steps with N; per-item scale = `usableWidth / columns(count)` ŌĆö never a constant. Hard-cap the drawn count (100) and carry a `1/factor` correction so displayed numbers stay honest. Give the grid an explicit vertical budget (fraction of VIDEO_HEIGHT) with an overflow offset. Normalize data visuals to the per-render maximum inside a fixed track (tallest bar exactly fills it for every input), with a visibility floor for small values.
- **Fails when:** the correction factor is dropped (counts lie) or the vertical budget is omitted (grid invades the neighbor zone). This is the same idea as our `fitUnitsToZone` ŌĆö the vendors confirm the shape; adoption is our problem, not design.

### L9. Reflow-free animated text
- **Repos:** github-unwrapped (`vendor/github-unwrapped/remotion/SevenSegment/SevenSegmentNumber.tsx:33-35, 78-89`), remotion-superpowers (`vendor/remotion-superpowers/skills/remotion-production/rules/data-visualization.md:141,172`; `vendor/remotion-superpowers/skills/remotion-production/rules/animation-presets.md:195`), short-video-maker (`vendor/short-video-maker/src/components/videos/PortraitVideo.tsx:30-36`), recorder (`vendor/recorder/remotion/chapters/landscape/ChapterTimestamp.tsx:10-17,26-37`).
- **Recipe:** Lay out counters at their TERMINAL value from frame 0: pass `max`, reserve `String(max).length` digit cells (hidden leading zeros keep their cell), or at minimum `fontVariantNumeric: "tabular-nums"`. Size a recurring label box by measuring the WIDEST string it will ever hold, once. Per-word emphasis must be geometry-neutral: padding cancelled by equal negative margins (or outline/shadow), so the line never reflows as the highlight moves. Always `extrapolateLeft/Right: "clamp"` on interpolate so easing overshoot can't push elements out of bounds.
- **Fails when:** rarely ŌĆö this is cheap, pure insurance. The only cost is remembering it per primitive.

### L10. Bounded-overflow containment (hard budgets + clips + last-resort uniform shrink)
- **Repos:** template-audiogram (`vendor/template-audiogram/src/Audiogram/get-number-of-lines-for-text.ts:52-67`), vanta (`vendor/vanta/src/scenes/KineticText.tsx:54-68`), open-design (`vendor/open-design/apps/daemon/src/brands/engine/artifacts/deck.ts:25-33,83-88,198-216`; `vendor/open-design/apps/daemon/src/brands/engine/artifacts/_shared.ts:131-132`), claude-video-kit (`vendor/claude-video-kit/remotion/src/compositions/CaptionsLayer.tsx:26-62`).
- **Recipe:** Give every text region a hard line budget and derive its reserved height as `linesPerPage ├Ś lineHeight`; page by dropping oldest lines, inside `overflow: hidden` as belt-and-braces. Wrap reveal animations in `overflow:hidden` + animated `clipPath: inset()` so the reveal can never spill its own box ŌĆö measurement-free. As a LAST resort, measure natural DOM extent vs the frame post-layout and apply one uniform `transform: scale()` with a 2% safety margin ŌĆö but gate the scale against a legibility floor.
- **Fails when:** containment silently destroys content ŌĆö `text-overflow: ellipsis` truncation with no warning (remotion-cinematic scan C) and char-count heuristics that assume uniform glyph width (claude-video-kit C1) are the weak forms; clipping is a guard, not a fitting strategy.

### L11. Scale-corrected DOM measurement (composition pixels, not screen pixels)
- **Repos:** remotion (`vendor/remotion/packages/core/src/use-current-scale.ts:54-121`), skills (`vendor/skills/skills/remotion/rules/measuring-dom-nodes.md:10-30`), remotion-cinematic (`vendor/remotion-cinematic/src/editor/EditorOverlay.tsx:39-41, 78-82, 181-183`).
- **Recipe:** Remotion scales the composition container to fit the preview viewport, so any `getBoundingClientRect()` must be divided by `useCurrentScale()` (or `renderedWidth / logicalWidth` measured live) to recover composition-space values. Recompute per interaction, never cache a stale ratio.
- **Fails when:** omitted ŌĆö phantom sizes that vary with window zoom; the exact bug class our "client-rect px vs viewBox coords" law names. Our `measureHook` already handles this via `getScreenCTM()`; any future interactive bbox tool needs the same correction.

### L12. Render-and-inspect gates with declared-intent markers
- **Repos:** open-design (`vendor/open-design/plugins/_official/examples/hyperframes/SKILL.md:412-476`), claude-video-kit (`vendor/claude-video-kit/scripts/verify-shorts.mjs:30-34, 141-160`), remotion-superpowers (`vendor/remotion-superpowers/agents/post-producer.md:42-46,94-105`).
- **Recipe:** Run the real composition headless, seek to sample points, report true bounding-box overflow/off-canvas + WCAG contrast with timestamps and fix hints. Two named escape hatches distinguish intentional from accidental: `data-layout-allow-overflow` (mid-animation overflow OK) and `data-layout-ignore` (decorative, never audited). Add a cheap ffprobe pre-filter on the rendered MP4 (exact canvas dims, duration cap, scene-change density) BEFORE any expensive pass. Pace linting is separable from collision linting: flag tweens <0.2s, >2s, and >1s dead zones.
- **Fails when:** it's the ONLY defense ŌĆö remotion-superpowers' renderŌåÆAI-reviewŌåÆfix loop pays full render cost per iteration; use as verification behind construction (L3) and pre-render measurement (L1), not instead of them. This is the one vendor pattern that approaches our `lesson:check --measured`; the escape-hatch markers map to our `ALLOWED_OVERLAP_PAIRS` + `decoration` zone.

**Field disagreement ŌĆö measurement vs heuristics.** short-video-maker's char budgets (`lineMaxLength: 20/30`), github-unwrapped's digit-count step tables, and claude-video-kit's CJK char counting all substitute calibration for measurement ŌĆö and all three scans document the resulting fragility (any font/canvas change silently breaks the calibration). Verdict: measure (L1) wherever text is data-driven; keep char budgets only as fast pre-filters inside a frozen design space.

## 3. Voice-visual sync playbook

### S1. Measured audio is the master clock (calculateMetadata derives duration; nothing is declared)
- **Repos:** template-audiogram (`vendor/template-audiogram/src/Root.tsx:42-62`), template-tiktok (`vendor/template-tiktok/src/CaptionedVideo/index.tsx:29-39`), short-video-maker (`vendor/short-video-maker/src/short-creator/libraries/Kokoro.ts:31-36` ŌåÆ `src/components/root/Root.tsx:11-19`), skills (`vendor/skills/skills/remotion/rules/voiceover.md:26-91`), remotion-superpowers (`vendor/remotion-superpowers/skills/remotion-production/rules/voiceover-sync.md:70-90`; ordering law at `vendor/remotion-superpowers/skills/remotion-production/rules/production-pipeline.md:20-33`), claude-video-kit (`vendor/claude-video-kit/scripts/build-metadata.mjs:18-38, 57-77`), recorder (`vendor/recorder/remotion/calculate-metadata/calc-metadata.ts:10-50`), github-unwrapped (content-driven analog, `vendor/github-unwrapped/remotion/Main.tsx:42-76`).
- **Recipe:** Voiceover FIRST (voice ŌåÆ music ŌåÆ SFX ŌåÆ footage). Probe every clip's REAL duration (parseMedia `slowDurationInSeconds`, mediabunny `computeDuration()`, or ffprobe ŌĆö never hand-parse WAV headers; claude-video-kit documents that exact bug) and set `durationInFrames = ceil(sum ├Ś fps) + explicit tail` inside `calculateMetadata`, passing per-scene durations back as props. Co-locate a `getXDuration(props)` function with each scene component and have BOTH `calculateMetadata` and the `<Series.Sequence>` mount consume the same function ŌĆö one source, structurally unable to disagree.
- **Fails when:** duration is audio-only ŌĆö no vendor has a motion budget; ours (`max(narration+gap, motion) + tail`) is the stronger form. Also fails when the documented pattern is simply not called: editor-pro-max ships the rule and hardcodes every composition duration anyway (its scan C2).

### S2. Unit-local audio: one clip per utterance, mounted in its own Sequence
- **Repos:** remotion-superpowers (`vendor/remotion-superpowers/skills/remotion-production/rules/elevenlabs-advanced.md:95-120`), claude-video-kit (`vendor/claude-video-kit/remotion/src/Root.tsx:163-179`), short-video-maker (`vendor/short-video-maker/src/components/videos/PortraitVideo.tsx:72-101`), skills (`vendor/skills/skills/remotion/rules/transcribe-captions.md:25-68` ŌĆö "Transcribe each clip individually").
- **Recipe:** Never a continuous multi-utterance WAV. One TTS clip per cue/line/speaker, each in its own `<Sequence from durationInFrames>` alongside its visuals and captions, so a clip structurally cannot cross a unit boundary. Keep ASR timestamps in the unit's LOCAL coordinates and let `<Sequence>` nesting do the global translation ŌĆö sync code never touches master-timeline numbers. Set pacing at TTS-generation time (speed presets), never by stretching audio in composition.
- **Fails when:** the chaining math is inlined in JSX ŌĆö short-video-maker shipped scene `durationInFrames` that is actually the scene END frame, masked only by paint-over (its scan C1). The cumulative sum belongs in one reconcile step, exactly our Wave 3.5.

### S3. Word-timestamp ASR as the single timing artifact, with a quality gate
- **Repos:** remotion (`vendor/remotion/packages/install-whisper-cpp/src/transcribe.ts:96-116,167-185,330`; `vendor/remotion/packages/install-whisper-cpp/src/to-captions.ts:12-33`), template-tiktok (`vendor/template-tiktok/sub.mjs:40-58`), recorder (`vendor/recorder/scripts/captions/caption-file.ts:41,57-75`), skills (`vendor/skills/skills/remotion/rules/subtitles.md:8-24`), editor-pro-max (`vendor/editor-pro-max/scripts/transcribe.ts:21-59`), open-design (`vendor/open-design/plugins/_official/examples/hyperframes/references/transcript-guide.md:1-102`).
- **Recipe:** Offline, cached, versionable: 16kHz WAV ŌåÆ whisper.cpp with `tokenLevelTimestamps: true` (DTW alignment: `--dtw <model>` + `--max-len 1` so every item is one token owning its own timestamp) ŌåÆ normalize immediately into ONE ms-based `Caption {text, startMs, endMs, timestampMs, confidence}` interchange type; frames appear only at the Sequence boundary. Scrub filler tokens (`[BLANK_AUDIO]`, `[PAUSE]`) in ONE shared function used by BOTH timing derivation and display (recorder). Gate transcript quality mechanically before use: music-token ratio >20%, garbled words, spans <0.05s ŌåÆ retry ladder small.en ŌåÆ medium.en ŌåÆ escalate to human (open-design).
- **Fails when:** ASR text is trusted for display ŌĆö claude-video-kit's hybrid (Whisper supplies TIMING, authored `voice_text` supplies TEXT, `vendor/claude-video-kit/scripts/align.py:112-186`) is the right fallback shape when ASR mis-transcribes proper nouns, though weaker than true per-token binding.

### S4. Token-onset binding for every spoken-synced visual (the karaoke law)
- **Repos:** template-tiktok (`vendor/template-tiktok/src/CaptionedVideo/Page.tsx:32,67-87`), template-audiogram (`vendor/template-audiogram/src/Audiogram/Word.tsx:11-30`), short-video-maker (`vendor/short-video-maker/src/components/videos/PortraitVideo.tsx:128-146`), skills (`vendor/skills/skills/remotion/rules/display-captions.md:135-172`), editor-pro-max (`vendor/editor-pro-max/src/components/text/CaptionOverlay.tsx:108-161,210-230`), recorder (`vendor/recorder/remotion/captions/boxed/components/SingleCaption.tsx:24,51-56`).
- **Recipe:** Inside a unit-local `<Sequence>`, reconstruct absolute time (`page.startMs + (frame/fps)*1000`) and mark a token active by half-open interval on MEASURED ms: `token.fromMs <= t && token.toMs > t` (strict upper bound avoids double-active at boundaries). No step constants, no even grids ŌĆö highlight cadence IS the speech cadence. Subtle ordering rule worth copying: anchor the END of a fixed-length color fade at the token onset (`interpolateColors` over `[startMs-100, startMs]`) so the word is fully lit exactly when spoken.
- **Fails when:** a fixed cadence sneaks in ŌĆö the skills repo's own demo assets use `CHAR_FRAMES = 2` and `HIGHLIGHT_START_FRAME = 30` literals one file away from its word-timestamp machinery (its scan C). **Disagreement, resolved:** remotion-superpowers promotes ~150 WPM frame budgeting as its PRIMARY scene-sync mechanism (`vendor/remotion-superpowers/skills/remotion-production/rules/voiceover-sync.md:32-48`) while measured Whisper timestamps sit one rule-file over. Side taken: WPM/char-rate estimates are drafting heuristics for WRITING to a budget (our Wave 2b usage); any RENDER-time sync from an estimate is the anti-pattern ŌĆö a 10% TTS pace deviation compounds to seconds by the last scene. Measure.

### S5. Time-budget caption paging with successor-clamped Sequences
- **Repos:** remotion (`vendor/remotion/packages/captions/src/create-tiktok-style-captions.ts:25-97`), template-tiktok (`vendor/template-tiktok/src/CaptionedVideo/index.tsx:110-131`), remotion-superpowers (`vendor/remotion-superpowers/skills/remotion-production/rules/captions-workflow.md:54-67,73-120`), short-video-maker (`vendor/short-video-maker/src/components/utils.ts:58-78`).
- **Recipe:** Fold word timestamps into display pages with ONE density knob (`combineTokensWithinMilliseconds`, 800-1200ms) ŌĆö no hand-authored caption cues. Mount each page as its own `<Sequence>` whose end is `Math.min(nextPage.startMs, page.startMs + budget)` converted to frames ŌĆö temporal non-overlap by construction; drop zero/negative pages. Key the entrance spring to the page-LOCAL frame so pop-in re-fires on every audio-derived boundary with zero timing code. Close a page when the next word starts >1s after the current page ends, so captions VANISH during pauses instead of stretching across them. Round frames (`Math.round`) ŌĆö always.
- **Fails when:** units mix ŌĆö template-tiktok added 1200 (ms) to a FRAME count, turning a 1.2s cap into a 40s no-op (its scan C1: textbook argument for typed time units); fractional frames drift boundaries; CJK text never contains the space that triggers a page break (remotion scan C3).

### S6. Speech-derived boundaries + adaptive silence handling
- **Repos:** recorder (`vendor/recorder/remotion/calculate-metadata/get-start-end-frame.ts:6-7,9-40,86-115`), editor-pro-max (`vendor/editor-pro-max/scripts/detect-silence.ts:26-73`; `vendor/editor-pro-max/src/utils/editing.ts:3-40`), skills/remotion (`vendor/skills/skills/remotion/rules/silence-detection.md:14-70`).

### Where the field disagrees with itself ŌĆö sides taken

1. **Estimate vs measure timing.** WPM/char-rate budgeting (`vendor/remotion-superpowers/skills/remotion-production/rules/voiceover-sync.md:32-48`) vs measured ASR/probed durations (everyone else). **Side: measure.** Estimates are writing-budget heuristics only; a render-time estimate compounds to seconds of drift.
2. **Dynamic ducking vs offline pre-attenuation.** Per-frame duck envelopes (S7) vs library-wide `loudnorm,volume=0.1` (short-video-maker `src/scripts/normalizeMusic.ts:10-19`). **Side: both, layered.** Offline normalization is the floor; only a dynamic duck opens up in narration gaps. Neither substitutes for an LUFS gate ŌĆö which no vendor has.
3. **Prevention vs detection for overlap.** Construction-first doctrine (L3/L5) vs render-and-inspect (L12). **Side: both, ordered.** Prevention is the norm-setter; measurement is the verifier that catches the exceptions (springs overshoot, residuals go negative). Either alone has documented failures.
4. **Measurement vs calibration for text.** `fitText`/`fillTextBox` (L1) vs char budgets and digit step tables (short-video-maker, github-unwrapped, claude-video-kit). **Side: measurement.** Every calibration-based repo's own scan documents the fragility; budgets survive only in frozen design spaces.
